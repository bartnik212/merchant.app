GAME RULES:


In the game, you control a resource trader. Resources can only be obtained by stealing from the company that produces the given raw material. After obtaining a given resource, you can sell it to the appropriate NPC. There are 3 types of resources:

WOOD IRON COPPER

POSSIBLE INTERACTIONS:


BATTLE WITH NPC CONVERSATION WITH NPC MERCHANT SALE OF RESOURCE CITY CHANGE REST FOR GENERATION PURCHASE OF WEAPON SELECTING OF WEAPON PLAYER STATUS VIEW

CITIES TO CHOOSE:

GDANSK - sell wood and obtain copper WARSAW - sell copper and obtain iron ZAKOPANE - sell iron and obtain wood

3 TYPES OF WEAPON AVAILABLE:

TWO HANDED SWORD SWORD AND SHIELD FIRE ARM

FIGHTS TAKE PLACE ON THE BASIS OF THE GAME "STONE PAPER SCISSORS"


FIRE ARM BEATS TWO HANDED SWORD TWO HANDED SWORD BEATS SWORD AND SHIELD SWORD AND SHIELD BEAT FIRE ARM

You don't know what weapon is fighting your opponent until you start fight with him


PLAYER WINS THE GAME WHEN HE OBTAINS 100 COINS AND LOSES WHEN HEALTH POINTS ARE 0
